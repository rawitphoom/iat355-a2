Assignment 3 - VegaLite Visualizations

Chart A – Global Sales by Genre & Platform

Stacked bars show how platforms contribute to each genre’s global sales. Action and Sports dominate overall. Nintendo (Wii/DS) and Sony’s PlayStation lines carry large shares, while PC is steady across several genres.

Chart B – Sales Over Time by Platform & Genre

Faceted line charts (2000–2016) show platform waves across genres—PS2/PS3 surges in Action/Role-Playing, a Wii spike around 2008 for family-friendly genres, and the broad post-2010 decline.

Chart C – Regional Sales vs Platform (grouped bars)

Grouped bars compare NA/EU/JP/Other by platform. NA and EU lead for most platforms, while JP skews toward Nintendo/PlayStation compared to Xbox.

Chart D - Japanese Sales Trends by Genre (2000–2020)

This line chart highlights Japan’s strong preference for Nintendo titles, especially in the Action and Role-Playing genres. The chart shows a consistent dominance of handheld systems (DS, 3DS, Switch) from 2005 onwards, compared to declining sales on Western consoles such as Xbox.
