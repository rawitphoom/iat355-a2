Assignment 3 - VegaLite Visualizations

Chart 1 - Simple Inline Bar Chart

A small test chart created directly in the JavaScript file using inline values. It demonstrates how VegaLite renders categorical data into a bar chart (categories A, B, C with amounts).

Chart 2 - Global Sales by Genre

This chart uses videogames_wide.csv to show total global sales grouped by game genre. Bars are sorted by total sales, highlighting how Action and Sports genres dominate the market.

Chart 3 - Regional Sales Trends Over Time

A stacked area chart using videogames_long.csv, showing how sales evolved from 1980–2020. Each region’s contribution is stacked to reveal market trends, with peaks around 2007–2009 and a noticeable decline after 2010.

Chart 4 - Top 10 Publishers by Global Sales

Horizontal bars make it easy to compare publisher totals. Aggregating Global_Sales and showing only the top 10 highlights who dominates the market overall.

Chart 5 - Regional Sales Intensity by Year

A heatmap shows where sales were strongest over time across regions. Darker cells indicate higher total sales (sum of sales_amount) in that year/region.

Chart 6 - NA vs EU Sales (size = Global Sales)

Each dot is a game (2000–2020). The x-axis shows sales in North America, the y-axis shows sales in Europe. Dot size encodes global sales, and color indicates genre. Look for clusters and outliers to see which genres and titles perform strongly in both regions.
